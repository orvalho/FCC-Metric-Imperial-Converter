Metric/Imp Converter

Description

If you need to convert from Imperial to Metric, or the other way around, you are at the right place!

You can convert:

  • gal <—> l
  • lbs <—> kg
  • mi <—> km

Example Usage

/api/convert?input=4gal /api/convert?input=1/2km /api/convert?input=5.4/3lbs /api/convert?input=kg

Example Output

{initNum: 3.1,
initUnit: 'mi',
returnNum: 4.98895,
returnUnit: 'km',
string: '3.1 miles converts to 4.98895 kilometers'}
